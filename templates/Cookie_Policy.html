RealHome uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized services. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes, including:
