What are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device by websites. They help with things like remembering login details, keeping track of your shopping cart, and personalizing your browsing experience. There are different types, including session cookies (temporary) and persistent cookies (longer-term). Some cookies come from the site you're visiting (first-party), while others come from external sources (third-party).
